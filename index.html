The Studio

Crafted with intention,
built to last

Founded in 2020 in Halifax's North End, Rough Cuts Woodworking Studio was born from a deep respect for natural wood and the belief that everyday objects deserve to be beautiful. Each piece that leaves our shop is handcrafted with care, sealed with premium Odie's Oil, and stamped with our custom branding iron.

From a single end-grain cutting board to a full live edge dining table, we approach every project with the same attention to detail. Whether you're looking for the perfect gift, a statement piece for your home, or branded items for a corporate event, we'll bring your vision to life.

Live Edge Tables

Statement dining and coffee tables that celebrate the natural contours of the wood. Each slab is carefully selected, finished, and paired with custom steel or wood bases.

Cutting & Charcuterie Boards
